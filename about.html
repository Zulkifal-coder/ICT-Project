About Us

A small place with a clear idea.

GreenBite Serve good food and good coffee in a calm, welcoming room. We work with local farmers, change our menu with the seasons, and try to do a few things very well rather than many things at once.

Our Kitchen

Our chefs cook with what's fresh vegetables from nearby farms, fish from the coast, herbs from our own garden. Recipes lean Mediterranean, but the spirit is always seasonal and unfussy.

Our Cafe

We pull espresso from a small roaster two blocks away, bake pastries each morning, and brew tea by the pot. Whether you stop in for a quick coffee or stay the afternoon with a book, the table is yours.

Our Space

Plants, wood, soft light. We built GreenBite to feel like a quiet corner of the neighborhood somewhere to slow down for an hour or two.
